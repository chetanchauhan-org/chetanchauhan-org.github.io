The Foundation

The ‘Chetan Chauhan Foundation’ is a registered non-profit organisation dedicated to carrying forward the vision and values of Late Shri Chetan Chauhan – a legendary cricketer, statesman, and mentor. The Foundation is committed to empowering youth – both girls and boys – through the medium of sports, while also fostering good health, mental alertness, and holistic development.

Smt. Sangeeta Chetan Chahuhan

The Foundation is chaired by Shri Chauhan’s wife, Smt. Sangeeta Chetan Chauhan, and ably supported by their son, Shri Vinayak Chauhan.
Our core mission is to promote inclusiveness, teamwork, and camaraderie among young people, shaping them into responsible citizens and future leaders. We aim to provide equal opportunities for youth to face life’s challenges by instilling social values, building confidence, and creating avenues for academic and sporting excellence.

Our Vision

We are dedicated to fostering a world where every individual has the opportunity to participate in sports, experience its transformative power, and achieve their full potential. Our mission is to build a more inclusive and healthier society through athletic engagement.

Founded by visionary leader Smt. Sangeeta Chauhan the organization champions the spirit of sportsmanship and personal development. We believe in the power of sports to break down barriers and create lasting positive change.

Our founder's message is one of unwavering commitment to the youth and the promotion of healthy lifestyles. We strive to create platforms where talent can flourish and dreams can take flight, ensuring a brighter future for all.

Our Key Objectives

To achieve these objectives, the Chetan Chauhan Foundation undertakes various
programs and activities, including:
Sports Events

Organizing sports events across urban and rural regions to promote participation
and talent development.

Awards

Instituting Awards for the recognition, encouragement, and support of young
athletes in diverse sporting disciplines.

Scholarships

Providing Academic Scholarships to deserving students to ensure education for all.

Camps

Conducting Talent Scouting Camps in rural areas to identify and nurture future
champions.

Sports Centres

Setting up Support Centers for talented but economically challenged children.

Competitions

Structuring and hosting competitions at School, College, and University levels in multiple sports categories.
